Therefore, fear not, little flock; do good; let earth and hell combine against you, for if ye are built upon my rock, they cannot prevail.

Doctrine and Covenants 6:34

We are all members of the Lord’s little flock, and we are special to Him. Life will be hard, but as we lean on Him, we will not fall.

Reflection

What is an experience you have had or have heard where someone had earth and hell combine against them, and received strength from the Lord? Write your thoughts down and share in a group if you feel comfortable.

Activity: Willow in the Wind

Works best with 6-8 people

  • Have someone volunteer to be the “willow”. Everyone else forms a circle around them.
  • The willow closes their eyes and lets the group know they are ready to fall.
  • The group lets the willow know they are ready to catch them.
  • With extended arms, the circle works to keep the willow upright.
  • Change who is the willow every 2-3 minutes
